1

Weight vs. Full score — what's the difference?

These two numbers are independent of each other.

Full score

The total points on the paper. What you score out of.

Quiz Aout of 100 pts
Quiz Bout of 10 pts
Weight

How much it counts toward your final grade. Totally separate.

Quiz A weight10%
Quiz B weight90%
Key idea: A quiz with 100 pts full score at 10% weight matters less than a quiz with 10 pts full score at 90% weight. The full score is just the marking scale — the weight is the real power.
Example A
Full score100 pts
Weight10%
You scored80 / 100
80% × 10% = 8 pts toward grade
Example B
Full score10 pts
Weight90%
You scored8 / 10
80% × 90% = 72 pts toward grade

4

Transmuted grade & letter grade

Raw scores are converted to the 75–100 institutional scale.

Official formula
Transmuted = 75 + ( (Raw − 50) ÷ 50 × 25 )
75 = minimum pass grade 50 = minimum passing raw score 25 = grade range (75→100)
